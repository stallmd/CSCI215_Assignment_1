Jake Dash

Jake Dash is the main character of the book. He is a ten year old kid who is a nationally ranked racecar driver. He loves racing. Jake admits that competing against Luke Kemia in the National Extremist Pro Race was the toughest race of his life. But, Jake said he knew he could win with his family, pit crew, and fans cheering him along the whole way. Jake plans to continue racing for the rest of his career!
