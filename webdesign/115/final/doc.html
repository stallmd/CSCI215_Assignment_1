Doc

Doc is Jake Dash's pit crew leader. He directs and helps Jake out while he's racing. During the Extremist Pro Race, Doc was the one who helped repair Jake's car after Luke Kemia filled the car with toxic chemicals. Doc has always been there for Jake to make sure he can be the best racecar driver around. In his sparetime, Doc even researches how he can make Jake's car better and faster!
