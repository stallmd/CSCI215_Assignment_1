About the Illustrator

Madison Fabian

Madison Fabian was born and grew up in Mt.Pleasant South Carolina. He will be graduating the College of Charleston in 2018 with a major completed in Studio Art. After graduation Madison plans to actively pursue is career as an artist. He plans to establish his name by his pieces placed in local galleries in Charleston and through his efforts of being a children's book illustrator. When the author, Mackenzie Stall, was searching for a illustrator through the school, Madison Fabian was specifically recommended by the Chair of the Art Department at CofC. Madison put many hours into the creation of the characters in The Race of a Lifetime, making sure every detail of the characters paralleled the journey of a child undergoing Leukemia. This is the first book Madison has illustrated.
